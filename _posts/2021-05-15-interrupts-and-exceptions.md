title: Interrupts and Exceptions
---

Interrupts are used to handle external events,   
exceptions are used to handle instruction faults,   
both of them alter the program flow.

Here are some useful materials:   
[Difference between Interrupt and Exception](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/difference-between-interrupt-and-exception/)
