title: The Road Not Taken
---

There are two roads,   
which one do I choose?   
what if I choose wrong?

There is no absolutely right or wrong choice,   
only decision, action, awakening and movement,   
growth and engagement are very important.

It is difficult to make a decision,   
I took the one less traveled by,   
and that has made all the difference.   
[I am proud of my decison](https://www.poetryfoundation.org/poems/44272/the-road-not-taken).
